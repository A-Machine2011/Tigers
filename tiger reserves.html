Top 3 Best Tiger Reserves in India

Name Locatioin Fun Fact
Jim Corbett Tiger Reserve Uttarakhand Jim Corbett was the legendary hunter that the park was named after. It's considered the first National Park in India.
Ranthambore Tiger Reserve Rajasthan It's one of the largest of India's National Park covering an area of 1,134 sq km. This tiger reserve was one of the most sought out after hunting destination of the royal Jaipur Maharajas.
Sunderban Tiger Reserve West Bengal The Royal Bengal tiger is kept in Sunderband. It also houses lots of endangered species of aquatic ammals and reptiles!
